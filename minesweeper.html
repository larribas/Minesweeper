How to play:

This game consists of a field with a number of mines buried randomly. As a bomb disposal expert, your work is to deactivate them. To do so, you have to flag the mined squares (right-clicking them), being careful to make the right choice -since you only have as many flags as there are bombs. The other squares indicate the number of mines they are surrounded by (up to 8).
